## Brand Overview
1914 is an essential oil brand I created during alternative military service. I handled the full process independently: naming, logo and visual identity, packaging design, fragrance formulation, and customer service automation. The Lab combines biochemistry, design, and engineering into one coherent brand experience.

## Product & Experience
- Develop fragrance formulas with a scientific approach, emphasizing stability and safety
- Create a consistent visual system: logo, packaging, and marketing materials
- Build 24/7 customer service flows with LINE and Messenger chatbots

## Stack
- Frontend: VanillaJS
- Chatbot: LINE, Messenger + Google Dialogflow for semantic understanding
- Deployment: Heroku
- Design: UI/UX, branding, and graphic design with Photoshop
