# Next.js 15 in Production

## Content pipeline

Velite generates type-safe collections at build time — a good fit for marketing + blog hybrids.

## Performance

- Static homepage + ISR where needed
- Images via `next/image` or prebuilt OG assets
- Keep client bundles lean — prefer CSS and restrained motion

## SEO

Ship RSS, sitemap, and JSON-LD on launch day, not as a later patch.
