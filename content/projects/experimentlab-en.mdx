## Overview
ExperimentLab is a prototype platform designed to help physicians prepare research proposals. It uses indicator data such as dialysis-related metrics and gene expression signals to build early prediction models and evaluate disease progression or recovery trends.

## Feature Focus
- Visualize indicator data and track trend changes
- Build baseline model estimates with adjustable parameters
- Record and export reports for research workflows

## Stack
- Frontend: Vue 3
- Design: UI/UX design, data visualization
