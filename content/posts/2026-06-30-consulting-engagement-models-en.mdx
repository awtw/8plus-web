# Consulting Models

## Advisory Retainer

Best when you have engineers but need senior architecture and ongoing review. Lower risk, steady cadence.

## Project Delivery

Best for new products or rewrites needing end-to-end ownership. Scope milestones and acceptance criteria matter.

## Workshop

Best for AI alignment, architecture workshops, skill building. Deliver action items, not one-way training.
