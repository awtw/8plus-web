# B18 Brand Website

**Tags**: vibe coding, Next.js, Instagram, UI/UX Design, Vercel

## Overview
This Lab is a brand website for an emerging clothing designer, focused on visual language, brand storytelling, and social touchpoints that guide users toward interaction and conversion.

## Design & Experience
- Use layout and whitespace to highlight product texture and brand positioning
- Guide users from brand discovery to social and purchase flows
- Keep a consistent design system and reusable components

## Stack
- Frontend: Next.js
- Deployment: Vercel
- Social integration: Instagram embed / traffic routing
