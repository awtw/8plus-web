## Overview
This is a new-generation personal portfolio built quickly by combining years of experience with a modern design language. It integrates a Google Calendar booking flow so people interested in consulting can book directly online and reduce conversion friction.

## Highlights
- Clear information architecture that emphasizes Lab value and role contribution
- Layout and motion details centered around readability
- Built-in booking entry point with Google Calendar

## Stack
- Framework: Next.js
- Deployment: Vercel
- Integration: Google Calendar API
