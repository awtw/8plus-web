## Scope
This Lab explores multiple CRM prototype directions across common back-office modules: authentication, role-based access, list search, form editing, and guided onboarding workflows.

## Design Focus
- Consistent visual language and reusable component patterns
- Task-oriented page flows and interaction paths
- Scalable list and form structures with permissions and state variations

## Stack
- Frontend: VanillaJS, Next.js
- Deployment: Vercel
- Design: UI/UX design, component thinking
