## Overview
Built from years of product and engineering experience, 8plus is a booking-first consulting platform for deeper conversations around career planning and software development. It combines analytics thinking with practical engineering experience to help users identify strengths and map actionable next steps.

## Highlights
- Systematic booking flow with Google Calendar integration
- Goal-centered consulting framework and question prompts
- Minimal, clear interface designed to reduce friction and increase completion rate

## Stack
- Frontend: Next.js
- Deployment: Vercel
- Integration: Google Calendar API
- Design: UI/UX and interaction polish
